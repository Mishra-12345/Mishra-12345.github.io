Welcome to AntiVir Database

AntiVir is a manually curated database of antiviral plants that compiles comprehensive information on plant species, their taxonomic families, associated phytochemicals, and virus-specific antiviral activity.

The database integrates experimentally reported antiviral evidence to provide structured, searchable, and statistically summarized data for plant-based antiviral research.

Since the antiviral property of plants is mainly due to active, hence we thought of including them as well in our database. In depth analysis of active compounds present in medicinal plants revealed that CRUDE EXTRACT consisting of either ethanol, methanol or aqueous extract possessed antiviral activity.
Distribution of active compounds
Sorting on the active compound revealed that there were numerous naturally acting compounds that possessed antiviral activity. Interestingly, there were some compounds which had the potential to inhibit diverse viruses suggesting their potential candidature into drug development.
