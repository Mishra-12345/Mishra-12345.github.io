Welcome to AntiVir Database

AntiVir is a manually curated database of antiviral plants that compiles comprehensive information on plant species, their taxonomic families, associated phytochemicals, and virus-specific antiviral activity.

The database integrates experimentally reported antiviral evidence to provide structured, searchable, and statistically summarized data for plant-based antiviral research.
